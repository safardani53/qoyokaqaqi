Early Life of Travis Scott

Born on 30 April 1991, Travis Scott’s age is 31 Years Old as of 2022. He grew up in Houston, Texas, US.

Until he was six years old, was living in South Park, Houston, with his grandmother. The neighborhood was well-known for criminal activities and had a high crime rate.

The surrounding scenes had a negative impact on Scott, and he had seen crazy shitty activities there. He claims that those activities and behavior of people made him who he is today.

Later, he shifted to Missouri City. It was a middle-class suburban area that formed the border of southwest Houston. Here he lived with his parents.

His mother was an employee at Apple and his father was the owner of a business. Travis’s father was also a musician and his grandfather was known for his jazz composition.

Travis went to Elkins High School and graduated when he was 17 years old. He had a keen interest and participated in musical theatre, during his high school days.

Early beginnings in music

Scott went to the University of Texas at San Antonio for higher study but dropped out when he was in his second year to pursue a music career.

Travis Scott teamed up with his friend, Chris Holloway, formed The Graduates, and released Myspace, an untitled EP, which was his first project, in 2008. He also formed a group with schoolmate OG Chess, named The Classmates.

Travis Scott also mentioned that he was very much influenced by Kid Cudi, Bon Iver, M.I.A., Tame Impala, T.I., Kanye West, Toro y Moi, and Thom Yorke. Scott’s mixtape called the Owl Pharaoh was also compared to Kid Cudi’s album called the Man on the Moon II: The Legend of Mr. Rager, by Spin magazine.

Travis Scott is mainly influenced by Alex Tumay and Mike Dean and used audio manipulations in his songs. The effects he used were stereo-sculpted chorusing, auto-tune, harmony structures, phasing, and delays.

Travis Scott Career

Travis Scott is one of the most successful rappers in Hollywood. He began his music career in the year 2008 when he collaborated with one of his friends named Chris Holloway.

They released an EP without any name and were loved by people. After this also, he collaborated with his friends but he shot to fame in the year 2012 as he secured a contract with Epic Records and also published “Good Music”.

This piece of work gave him much-awaited fame. Later on, in the year 2013, his first ever mixtape was released named “Owl Pharaoh”. From there onwards, he never looked back and gave many hits in a series.

In the year 2015, his first-ever album was released named “Rodeo”.And the following year, his one more album was released named Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight and it became a massive hit among the fans.

In addition to this, the Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight album released in the year 2016 gained the first position in the Billboard 200.

The Classmates

He founded the ensemble “The Classmates” in 2009 with his former classmate OG Chess.

The Classmates released two projects in 2009 and 2010. The second was with Cruis’n USA, while the first was with Buddy Rich.

Scott focused mostly on the projects’ production aspects. The pair remained a unit until 2012 when they broke up because of personal and financial issues.

The mixtape “Owl Pharaoh” by Scott was his first-ever full-length work. Call it fate or bad luck, but the project’s release was postponed, and Mike Dean and Kanye West later remade it.

A music video for the song “Quintana,” which was slated to be in “Owl Pharaoh,” was published by Scott in March 2013. Scott joined the 2013 Freshman Class of the XXL during this period.

Rise To Prominence

Scott released his song “Don’t Play” in July 2014 as the lead single from his major label debut studio album, “Days Before Rodeo.”

The song was a huge success. Scott announced a concert tour called “The Rodeo Tour” with rapper Young Thug of the Rich Gang and producer Metro Boomin as a result of this success.

Scott’s eagerly anticipated album “Rodeo” was released in September 2015. Several artists made cameo appearances in it.

The album debuted at number three on the US Billboard 200 chart and was an immediate success. With the top spot on the Billboard Rap Album chart, “Rodeo” hit the mark.

Two singles from his album “Rodeo,” “3500” and “Antidote,” were released. While “3500” was a well-received success, his career took off with “Antidote.”

When it peaked at number 16, it became the single with the highest chart position on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Travis Scott has won many awards

Travis Scott is a successful rapper and therefore it is obvious that he must have received many accolades for his contribution to the music industry. Some of the major awards won by him for his work include.

  • Best Hip Hop Award for his work “Antidote” in 2016.
  • In 2017, he won Teen Choice Award for his work “Know No Better”.
  • He won the BET award for his work “Sicko Mode” in 2019.
  • Again in the year 2019, he won Billboard Music Award for his work “Sicko Mode”.
  • In the year 2021, he won MTV Video Music Award for his work “Franchise”.
  • He was also nominated for the Grammy Awards many times but could not get the award. However, in the year 2020, he was awarded the Latin Grammy Award for TKN.

Major Success

He collaborated with Thug on the 2016 single “Pick Up The Phone,” which was published. Majorly popular, the song peaked at number 43 on the Billboard Hot 100.

The Recording Industry Association of America awarded it double platinum certification.

Three new songs by Scott, including “A Man,” “Green & Purple,” and “Butterfly Effect,” were made available on SoundCloud in May 2017.

He followed it up with a “DAMN Tour” and a performance of their song “Walk On Water” at the 2017 MTV Video Music Awards.

Scott was a featured artist on Trippie Redd’s song “Dark Knight Dummo” at the end of the year. On the Billboard Hot 100, it peaked at position 72.

His joint album with Quavo, “Huncho Jack, Jack Huncho,” was released a week later. Seven of the album’s tracks made the Billboard Hot 100 chart when it debuted at number three on the Billboard 200.

Travis Scott Personal Life  

Scott began dating media personality and businesswoman Kylie Jenner in April 2017. In February 2018, Jenner gave birth to their daughter.

Jenner appeared in the music video for “Stop Trying to Be God”, from Scott’s third studio album Astroworld.

They broke up in September 2019 but quarantined together during the COVID-19 pandemic for the sake of their daughter and ended up rekindling their relationship.

On September 7, 2021, after weeks of speculation, Jenner revealed that she and Scott were expecting their second child. Jenner gave birth to their son in February 2022.
