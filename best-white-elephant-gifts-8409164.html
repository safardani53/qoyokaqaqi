The 53 Best White Elephant Gifts

The holiday season is all about making memories with your loved ones through old and new traditions. One of our favorite traditions is the white elephant gift exchange, which is a fun and festive party game for friends and co-workers, as well as a budget-friendly gift-giving solution for large families. But finding the best white elephant gifts —  a.k.a. the ones that everyone wants to steal — can require a lot of creativity. 

If you don’t have time to plot and plan your gift idea this year, let us do the work for you. We researched dozens of holiday gift ideas across multiple categories and budgets to find the 53 best white elephant gifts to give this season.

Under $15 Gifts

Dash Mini Gingerbread Waffle Maker

Dash waffle makers are one of the best budget-friendly gift ideas, as most are under $10. This compact kitchen appliance is designed to create adorable breakfast waffles with a gingerbread imprint in just minutes.

30 Watt Silicone Wine Glass Holder

This silicone wine glass holder for the bath and shower is one of the best gift ideas for wine lovers, and it makes for a fun under-$15 gift, too. The holder comes in several different colors and is designed to suction to your shower wall for stability. Plus, it’s available on Amazon Prime, so it’s a great last-minute gift or stocking stuffer idea. 

Barbie Uno

Games are some of the best white elephant gift ideas, and this Barbie Uno deck is fun for adults and kids alike. The gift features characters from Barbie The Movie and comes with a special rule to switch up the classic card game. 

Target Beauty Capsule Black-Owned or Founded Beauty Sample Box Gift Set

‘Tis the season for gift sets, and Target has some of our favorite beauty options. The Target Beauty Capsule Black-Owned or Founded Beauty Sample Gift Set features seven products from Thank God It’s Natural, SheaMoisture, and Bevel, and costs under $15, making it a great white elephant gift idea. 

Gaiatop Mini Portable Fan

The Gaiatop Mini Portable Fan is the best gift for concerts, sporting events, and theme parks. Its compact size makes it easy to travel with, and its three-speed features allow you to customize the temperature. Plus, it comes with a stand, so you can sit it on your desktop or bedside table while traveling.

Wishing You Wellness Skincare Tool Gift Set

The Wishing You Wellness Skincare Tool Gift Set is another self-care gift idea not to pass up. This under-$15 gift comes with a jade roller, silicone face scrubber, and terry wristbands to keep arms and clothing dry while giving an at-home facial.

Baggu Baby Baggu

Baggu is one of the best places to buy holiday gifts. If you’re looking for an under-$15 gift idea that is actually useful, consider the Baby Baggu shopping tote, which is one of our favorite reusable shopping bags for the grocery store, farmer’s market, and beyond.

Gritin 9 LED Rechargeable Book Light

The Gritin 9 LED Rechargeable Book Light is one of the best Christmas gifts we’ve ever received — and it also makes for a great white elephant gift idea. This under-$15 gift illuminates your books, making it easier to read by the fireplace, on a plane, or in bed at night. Plus, it’s rechargeable so you never have to worry about batteries. 

E Ecsem Desktop Dust Vacuum Cleaner

White elephant gift exchanges are the best time to buy gifts people need but don’t necessarily ask for — like a miniature desktop vacuum. This cute desktop vacuum cleaner keeps your desk area spick and span, is an easy way to pick up hard-to-clean messes like glitter and sugar, and is a fun idea for office white elephant exchanges or co-worker gifts. 

Johanna Basford Worlds of Wonder

Give the gift of a mindfulness activity with the Johanna Basford Worlds of Wonder coloring book. This under-$15 gift idea features a collection of fantasy realms and intricate detailing designed to help you focus and relax.

Squishmallow Original 14-Inch Forina Green-Spotted Jellyfish

Squishmallows are the most popular plushes of 2024, and we understand why kids love them. They're super soft and squishy, making them the perfect companion for naps, bedtime, and lounging. Plus, they're collectable, and this jellyfish is currently discounted 33 percent.

Sugarfina Martini Olive Almonds

The Sugarfina Martini Olive Almonds are one of the best under-$10 white elephant gifts. These luxe candies are a delicious treat on their own but would also look adorable alongside a drinkware set (like these Oneida True Colors Cocktail Glasses) or paired with a martini kit (such as the Collins Dirty Martini Kit).

Under $25 Gifts

Carhartt Knit Cuffed Beanie

Beanies are another tried-and-true holiday gift idea to give this season. The Carhartt Knit Cuffed Beanie boasts a classic style, is available in a variety of colors, and is highly durable and effective.

We're Not Really Strangers Game

Open Story Neck Pillow & Sleep Mask Set

A Christmas Story Leg Lamp Night Light

Jenny Lemons Pickle Hair Claw Clip

Nod to holiday tradition — and humor — with this pickle claw clip from Jenny Lemons. The clip is constructed from durable acrylic and features a fun illustrative pickle that keeps hair in place. 

Tamagotchi Original

This holiday season, get nostalgic with the Tamagotchi Original keychain. This under-$20 gift idea features a classic Tamagotchi in 20 different colors, prints, and patterns. And, since it’s on Amazon Prime, it’s a great last-minute Christmas gift idea.
